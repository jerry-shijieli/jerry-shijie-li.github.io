After I finished my Master Degree in Physics from University of Nebraska-Lincoln, I stepped on my way of becoming a data scientist with strong quantitative research backgrounds and proficient software engineering skills.

Currently, I'm in my second year pursuing PhD degree in Computer Science on Data Science Track at North Carolina State University. This motivation originate from my lasting interest in building smart software to solve practical problems as well as my sense on the rapid development and application of artificial intelligence.

I'm now doing research with my advisor Dr. Nagiza Samatova in the BigData Science Lab. My research focus on the construction of information recommender systems with mainly text analytics as well as other machine learning technology. I am currently developing algorithms and software to combine cutting-edge technology into powerful tools that can provide information accurately and efficiently with excellent user experience.
